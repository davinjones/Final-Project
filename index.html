About the Artist

Education

Alexandra King studied at the precious Bellarmine University, majoring in Fine Art and minoring in Psychology.
"I believe it's important to learn from real artists and to really understand what your making.

Learn More About Art Education!

Mediums

Alexandra uses a multitude of mediums when creating art. These include, but are not limited to: Oil, Acrylic, Watercolor, Charcoal, Colored Pencil, Digital and Mixed Media.
"To become a great artist, you must be versatile. The art you create will take on many forms; you have to be ready to bring it to life"

Learn More About Art Mediums!

Work Ethic

Alexandra King believes that you 'get what you give'. If you want your art to reach the standard the greater art community expects, you have to put in the work. She has spent countless hours doing tedious exercises and putting her heart into everything she touches.
